About me

Mubashir Hanif is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of TechMatter, a healthcare technology and managed IT services company headquartered in California, USA, with operations in the US, UK, UAE, and East Asia. Since establishing the company in 2017, he has guided its transformation from a small team of 10 into a 500+ member enterprise delivering advanced healthcare and managed IT solutions to clients worldwide.

Mubashir Hanif is an ACCA-qualified business leader and technology entrepreneur who specializes in scaling digital health and IT services organizations through innovation, automation, and strategic growth.

TechMatter is a healthcare technology and managed IT services company based in Glendale, California. Founded in 2017 by Mubashir Hanif, the company specializes in enterprise software, automation, and revenue cycle management.

What I Do

At TechMatter, I lead strategy across platform development, acquisitions, and executive governance. The organization has grown into a multi-entity ecosystem serving healthcare providers and enterprise clients in regulated, high-accountability environments.

My work today spans:

  • Healthcare technology platforms and services
  • AI-enabled revenue and billing systems
  • Credentialing and compliance infrastructure
  • Data and operational intelligence
  • Enterprise managed IT services
  • Custom software development for complex environments

Growth, Scope, and Scalability

In 2025, we entered a decisive phase of growth with the acquisition of DoctorPapers and StateDrives, expanding our capabilities across documentation, compliance, and data infrastructure.

We also launched CureAR, our AI-enabled medical billing platform focused on accuracy, transparency, and financial visibility in healthcare operations.

Beyond healthcare, I’ve overseen enterprise IT and software engagements supporting institutions with strict reliability and security requirements, including NASA, Stanford University, and U.S. 911 Emergency Services operations.

Scale & Impact

TechMatter operates with:

  • 600+ employees globally
  • $4M+ in annual revenue
  • Active delivery across 4 regions (US, UK, Middle East, Asia)
  • A portfolio spanning services, platforms, and proprietary software

Our growth has been deliberate, built around long-term systems rather than short-term acceleration.

How I Lead

I lead with structure, clarity, and direct accountability.

In practice, that means

  • Open access to leadership and honest dialogue
  • Clear ownership of outcomes, not titles
  • Early identification of risk and inefficiency
  • Building teams that understand both why and how systems work

I believe technology should simplify work, strengthen trust, and hold up under pressure. That principle continues to guide how I build, lead, and grow TechMatter.
